About Us

Welcome to Aananda Academy

Aananda Academy is a trusted educational institute in Nepal dedicated to empowering students through quality education. We offer a wide range of courses designed to help individuals achieve their personal, academic, and professional goals.

From Loksewa preparation to life skills, our experienced faculty and supportive environment have helped hundreds of students build successful careers and fulfilling lives.

Our Inspiration

Yogi Bhupal Rokaya

Founder | Yoga Guru | English Language Specialist

Yogi Bhupal Rokaya is the visionary founder behind Aananda Academy. With deep expertise in Yoga, life sciences, and the English language, he has dedicated his life to transforming the traditional educational landscape of Nepal.

"Education is not just about gaining knowledge; it's about inner transformation and preparing students to face the world with confidence and character."

Under his leadership, Aananda Academy has pioneered the 'Bal Sanskar Kendra' concept in Nepal, integrating spiritual well-being with academic excellence. Whether leading a Yoga session or teaching advanced English, Yogi Bhupal's passion for teaching continues to inspire thousands of students.
